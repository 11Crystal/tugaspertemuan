About Us

Who We Are

Worldwide Trade Co. is an international trade facilitator based in Indonesia, focused on connecting foreign product suppliers with global markets. With over 10 years of experience, we provide import-export consulting, sourcing, and logistics for businesses of all sizes.

Our reputation is built on integrity, global reach, and a commitment to empowering local markets with the best global products.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become the leading platform that promotes and distributes high-quality foreign goods to emerging markets around the world.

Mission

To connect global manufacturers with regional demand through reliable trade channels, ethical partnerships, and consistent support.

Trust

We believe strong relationships are built on honesty and reliability.

Innovation

We adapt to global changes and bring the best products to market.

Global Impact

We support growth in local communities by connecting them to international opportunities.
