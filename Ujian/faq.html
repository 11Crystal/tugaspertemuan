Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

What types of products do you offer?

We specialize in importing high-quality goods from around the world, including kitchenware, skincare, gourmet food, and more.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We offer global shipping with reliable logistics partners to ensure your orders arrive safely and on time.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

Minimum quantities may vary depending on the product. Please check the individual product page or contact our support team for details.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number by email. You can use it to track your delivery status in real-time.

Do you provide product certifications?

Yes, we work with certified suppliers and can provide necessary documentation upon request.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfer, credit card, and selected digital payments depending on the region.

How can I get in touch with customer support?

You can reach our support team via the Contact page. We're available Monday to Friday during business hours.
